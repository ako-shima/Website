MANGO

Ally Mango

"Cebu's Ally Mango is a beloved mango shake shop renowned for its generous servings of large mango chunks, all at an affordable price. This popular spot stands out among other mango shops for offering more fruit in every shake, ensuring a rich and satisfying experience. You can find Ally Mango at IT Park's night market, Carbon Market, and Ayala Mall, making it easily accessible for both locals and visitors. With its unbeatable combination of quality and value, Ally Mango enjoys overwhelming popularity in Cebu, providing a delightful taste of the island's famed mangoes in every refreshing sip."

7D Dried Mangoes Dark Chocolate Enrobed

"Cebu's 7D Dried Mangoes are a beloved delicacy known for their exceptional quality, natural sweetness, and chewy texture. Made from the finest Cebu mangoes, they offer a perfect balance of sweetness and tanginess. For an extra indulgence, the 7D Chocolate-Covered Mangoes combine the luscious flavor of dried mangoes with a rich chocolate coating, creating a delightful fusion of fruity and chocolaty goodness. Widely available in local markets, souvenir shops, and malls across Cebu, these treats capture the essence of the island's renowned mangoes, offering a taste of tropical paradise in every bite. Whether enjoyed as a snack or shared as a gift, they provide a delicious way to experience Cebu's flavors."

Mango shaved ice in TOPS

"Cebu's mango shaved ice is a refreshing and delightful treat that perfectly captures the tropical essence of the island. Made with finely shaved ice topped with generous portions of fresh, sweet mango chunks, this dessert is both cooling and satisfying. The rich flavor of Cebu's renowned mangoes shines through in every bite, complemented by a drizzle of condensed milk or a scoop of mango ice cream for added indulgence. Available at various cafes and dessert shops around Cebu, mango shaved ice is a popular choice for beating the heat and enjoying a taste of the island's signature fruit. This dessert offers a perfect balance of creamy, sweet, and refreshing flavors, making it a must-try for anyone visiting Cebu."

Mango sticky rice

"Mango sticky rice is originally a traditional dessert from Thailand, but it's also available and enjoyed in Cebu. This dessert features sweet and sticky glutinous rice paired with ripe mango slices and creamy coconut sauce. While it's commonly found in Thai restaurants and street food stalls, it's also popular in Cebu, especially during mango season. Its allure lies in the perfect harmony of smooth coconut sauce and the sweetness of mango, creating a tantalizing flavor. The combination of mango flavor and sticky rice texture is irresistible, making it a must-try when visiting Cebu."
